## Inclusion Criteria
- Population: Adolescents (ages 10-19)
- Study type: Empirical studies (experimental, longitudinal, cross-sectional)
- Intervention: Prosocial video games (games that promote helping, cooperation, empathy)
- Outcome measures: Emotion regulation skills (self-report scales, behavioral measures, neural correlates)
- Publication years: 2000–present
- Language: English

## Exclusion Criteria
- Studies focusing on violent video games only
- Non‑empirical articles (reviews, editorials, theoretical papers)
- Studies with participants outside the 10‑19 age range
- Studies not reporting emotion regulation outcomes
- Non‑peer‑reviewed publications (preprints, conference abstracts without full data)
